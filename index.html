MOTIVATION

Leonardo da Vinci's statement "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication" highlights the idea that true elegance lies in simplicity. To achieve this, designers must often navigate complexities, distilling them down to their core elements. By doing so, we create designs that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional and memorable.

"Montage is the art of arranging time." A film immerses us in a carefully orchestrated sequence of time. Every shot, angle, and transition contributes to the overall story. The editor is the architect of this narrative, arranging these temporal fragments to create a cohesive and impactful experience.
